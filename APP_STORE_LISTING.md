**Recommended Description (1,247 characters):**

```
Daily Medicine Tracker - Simple & Reliable

Never miss a dose again. Medicine Tracker is the simplest way to record your daily medication intake with just one tap.

KEY FEATURES:
• One-button design - Tap once to record your medicine
• Automatic reset - Button refreshes at midnight every day
• Once-per-day limit - Prevents accidental double-tapping
• Private & secure - All data stored locally on your device
• Clean interface - No distractions, just what you need
• Visual feedback - Clear status and countdown to next reset

HOW IT WORKS:
1. Take your medicine
2. Tap the button once
3. Done! The button is disabled until midnight

The app automatically tracks the date and time of each dose. When you open the app, you'll see:
• Whether you've already taken your medicine today
• When you last confirmed taking your medicine
• Countdown until the button resets at midnight

PERFECT FOR:
• Daily medication reminders
• Tracking prescription compliance
• Simple habit tracking
• Anyone who needs a reliable daily medicine log

PRIVACY FIRST:
Your health data stays private. All information is stored locally on your device using iOS's secure storage. No accounts, no cloud sync, no data collection. Your privacy is our priority.

DESIGNED FOR SIMPLICITY:
We believe medication tracking shouldn't be complicated. No complex features, no overwhelming options - just a reliable way to record your daily medicine intake.

Download Medicine Tracker today and take control of your medication routine.
```

## Tips:

1. **Promotional Text**: This appears above your description in search results. Make it catchy and clear.

2. **Description**: 
   - Use the first 2-3 lines for impact (shown in search results)
   - Use bullet points for easy scanning
   - Include keywords naturally
   - Mention privacy (important for health apps)

3. **Keywords**: 
   - No spaces after commas
   - Use relevant terms people might search for
   - Don't repeat words from your app name
   - Focus on what users would actually search

4. **Update the placeholder URLs** in App Store Connect with real links (or remove Marketing URL if you don't have one)
